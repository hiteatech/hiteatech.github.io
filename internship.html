Event Summary

An internship is an opportunity offered by an employer to potential employees, called interns, to work at a firm for a fixed, limited period of time. In FTMK, Internship is taken during the forth year first semester, and it last for 6 months.

Internship is place where we can apply what we learnt for last 3 years in FTMK. We can develop our skills. There are so much benefits that can gain during the internship. Build out networks for near future when graduation time. We can call upon for advive and referrals. Gain Confidence in doing and completing task. Internships also act like a test drive. We often don't really what we really want, Internship in this stage can enable you take your career plan for a test drive.

However, finding a right internship is not easy. There are so much consideration to think before making a decision. We might sending tons of resume, and getting many good offers from several companies. How to pick a good one?

Therefore, we invited 4 of our FTMK seniors from multiple company and startup to share about their internship experience. So that, we can understand more on the working experience, the company background, the intern job scope and perhaps the allowance range.

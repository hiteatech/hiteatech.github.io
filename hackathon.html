About The Code

The Code is first ever collegiate hackathon hosted by FICTS. During our 24hour event, student developers and designer will gather on campus to buld and program ideas into reality. It doesn't matter if you're aiming to be the next Tony Stark or you're just looking to learn some code. The Code has something for you. No experience necessary. We invite you to come learn something new. In the same time, We bring the wifi, unlimited coffee, and mentors from some of the world's most influential companies. You bring a laptop, your creativity, and a desire to make a difference in a big way.

FAQ

What is Hackathon

A hackathon is a marathon software and hardware product creation event. Think of hacathons like the 100m spring of programming and coding. For 24 hours you and a small team will create something new and exciting. With many others team hacking alongside you.

I've never been to a hackthon and I'm nervous.

Have no fear - " " us first and foremost, a fun learning experience. We have beginner workshops held by industry professional. Our goal is for you to leave " " having learned something

Who can attend ?

All FTMK Students and Lecturers

What should I bring?

Bring your matric card. You'll also need your laptop and charger for hacking.

Will this cost me money ?

Nope. We'll provide all the food, swag and fun times you need to have an amazing weekend.

What if I don't know what I want to do?

We will be providing experienced people and ideas to help you with. We’ll also suggest ideas and themes leading up to the hackathon, as well as during the event. You are also welcomed to bring you course project or assignment here.

How do team work ?

We support teams of up to 4 members. If you want to apply as a group, first submit your individual application. Once all members of your planned team have submitted their individual applications, one you can complete this team form.

What if I don’t have a team?

You can decide whether we team you up or you can try and form one at the event!

I don't like team work? Can I solo?

Yes sure. It is all up to you as long as you can finish you work in 24 hours.

When do applications close?.

Applications close on May 13th at 23:59.

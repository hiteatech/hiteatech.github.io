Course Summary

Learn the basics of HTML and CSS, and take the first step on your journey to become a website developer!

This course is designed for students who are new to website development, and want to learn how to build a simple website.

Learning anything new can be tough. We will walk you through the process of designing a new website, but to get the most out of this course, you must bring your enthusiasm for learning, and budget time on your calendar to learn with us.

By the end of the course, you will build a simple website that you can share with your friends. We also hope that you will learn enough through this course to decide how best to continue your journey as a website developer, if you're interesting in pursuing such a path.

  • Prerequisites and Requirements

    1. Download
    - Sublime text or Atom
    - Materialize file and unzip it.
    2. Preferably place the folder at your desktop.
    3. Sleep enough.

  • What Will I Learn?

    List of learning outcome:

    1. The basic concept of HTML and CSS
    2. Create website with HTML, CSS
    3. Concept of framework
    4. Apply Materialize in your website
